We help people with Diabetes to overcome their challenges




DiabCare is a group of non for profit volunteers to provide support for people with Diabetes of all age groups to overcome their challenges through our workshops and events.


Our mission is to help people fight against diabetes and make them live a healthy and happy life


As per Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, 96 million people aged 18 years
or older have prediabetes. 26.4 million people aged 65 years or older have prediabetes.
Medical costs and lost work and wages for people with diagnosed diabetes total $327
billion yearly.



Quick Facts


  1. Type 1 diabetes is characterized by a lack of insulin production and type 2 diabetes results from the body’s ineffective use of insulin
  2. India has the world’s highest diabetes population with over 35 million people with diabetes. By 2025, this number is expected to swell to 70 million
  3. Approximately 17 million U.S. residents and 3 million Canadians have been diagnosed with diabetes
  4. As per Harvard school of public health, prediabetes and type 2 diabetes are completely preventable. About 9 in 10 cases can be avoided by making lifestyle changes which can also further lower the chances of developing cancer and cardio vasucular disease
  5. Did you know popular stars like Tom hanks, nick Jonas, randy Jackson and halle berry have openly declared they have diabetics condition.

Symptoms

  • Fatigue
  • Numbness or Tingling
  • Blurred Vision
  • Weight Loss

Causes

  • Insulin Resistance
  • Hormonal Diseases
  • Genes/Family History
  • Certain Medicines

Health Risk

  • Heart Disease & Stroke
  • Kidney Disease
  • Diabetic Neuropathy
  • Foot and Dental Problems
  • Eye Disease
