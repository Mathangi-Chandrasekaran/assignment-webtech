Our Reason




We are a group of peer support volunteers striving to help and support people with affordable access to Medicare and educate people about diabetics


. Through our donations we buy testing kits to screen people and help purchase insulin for free to ones who can’t afford the drug. We also organize different workshops to cope up and educate people treat diabetics.





People with diabetics often not seek medical attention due to cost associated with it and fear. Opposed to myth, diabetics is not life threatening and can be made dormant. As per WHO, diabetics is a chronic lifelong nature of the disease but its severity can be tempered down by following a healthy lifestyle and self-educating about it.

Its estimated that people from developing countries have significant portion of population having diabetics are undercounted due to lack of testing, and further ones tested either for Type1 or 2 diabetics don’t have means to afford proper medical attention due to high unaffordability towards Medicare .

Managing Diabetes

  • Eat healthy diet
  • Regular physical activity
  • Continous glucose monitoring
  • Prescribed Medicines

Insulin is a live saving drug that is commonly used to treat diabetics. Even though the drug itself is 100years old, the cost of it is heavy and as per WHO report its unaffordable to more than 75% of people from developing nations
